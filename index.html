What is ACM Student Chapter?

ACM Student Chapter is a local student organization affiliated with the Association for Computing Machinery (ACM), the world's largest educational and scientific computing society.

Who can join the ACM Student Chapter?

Any student enrolled at BVDU with an interest in computing, technology, or related fields can join our chapter.
